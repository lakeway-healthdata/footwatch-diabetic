Understanding Diabetic Foot Risks

Learn about the complications diabetes can cause and how proper foot care prevents serious problems

Nerve Damage

Diabetic neuropathy can reduce sensation in feet, making it difficult to detect injuries or pressure points.

Poor Circulation

Reduced blood flow can slow healing and increase infection risk, making foot injuries more serious.

Infection Risk

High blood sugar levels can impair immune function, making diabetics more susceptible to foot infections.

Delayed Healing

Diabetes can significantly slow the healing process, turning minor foot problems into major complications.
